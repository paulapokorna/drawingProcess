About

This long webscroll represents a creative common space network. It intends to promote sharing
and awareness of the collective responsibility of a digital environment that we leave behind for others.

Terms and Conditions


  • To make a drawing entry, continue building a drawing on top of the narratives of any previous entries.

  • Select one or multiple entries you wish to continue developing.

  • Draw anything that develops the narrative of selected pre-existing drawings.

  • Feel free to redraw existing entries and make any alterations.

  • Connect your drawing entry with those you chose to develop (node-like).
